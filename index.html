ABOUT US

As a collective of seasoned experts in the cultivation and trade of tangerines, our wealth of experience spans decades, allowing us an intimate understanding of what constitutes a superior tangerine. Thriving in a climate that mirrors paradise on Earth, Tangerine boasts the ideal conditions for cultivating these essential citrus gems. Our orchards in this citrus haven benefit from the perfect combination of temperate climate, well-draining loamy soil rich in organic matter, and an environment that fosters optimal growth. Much like Kashmiri apples, Tangerines from our region exhibit a distinctive allure, presenting a unique fusion of appearance, taste, fragrance, size, and color that sets them apart on the global stage.

VARIETIES

Clementine Tangerine

Clementine tangerines, originating in Morocco, have been cherished for centuries and are now extensively cultivated in Spain , the United States.

Dancy Tangerine

The Dancy tangerine, a citrus classic, has delighted for centuries since its discovery in late 1800s Florida.

Honey Tangerine

The Murcott/Honey Tangerine is a type of citrus tangerine that is known for its sweet and juicy fruits.

Pixie Tangerine

Pixie tangerines are a unique variety of tangerine that were first developed in California in the 1920s.

Satsuma Tangerine

Satsuma tangerines are a special variety of tangerines that originated in Japan but have since become popular around the world.

Yosemite Gold Tangerine

Yosemite Gold tangerines are a unique variety of citrus fruit native to California.

BENEFITS

Rich in Vitamin C: Tangerines are a potent source of vitamin C, which supports immune function, promotes skin health, and acts as an antioxidant. Antioxidant Properties: Loaded with antioxidants like flavonoids and carotenoids, tangerines help combat oxidative stress and protect the body from free radical damage. Dietary Fiber: Tangerines contain dietary fiber, aiding digestion, promoting gut health, and helping regulate blood sugar levels. Natural Hydration: With a high water content, tangerines contribute to hydration, supporting overall well-being and skin health. Low in Calories: Tangerines are relatively low in calories, making them a healthy and satisfying snack option for weight management. Rich in Nutrients: They provide essential nutrients like vitamin A, potassium, and folate, contributing to vision health, electrolyte balance, and cellular function.
